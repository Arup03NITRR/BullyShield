About BullyShield

BullyShield is an innovative platform dedicated to detecting and preventing cyberbullying in online spaces. Our mission is to create a safer digital environment for everyone, especially young internet users.

Using advanced machine learning algorithms, BullyShield analyzes text content to identify potentially harmful or bullying language in real-time. Our system can detect various forms of cyberbullying, including harassment, hate speech, and threatening language with 94% accuracy.

How It Works

  1. Enter or paste the text you want to analyze
  2. Our AI processes content using advanced NLP techniques
  3. Instant bullying risk assessment with detailed explanations
  4. Actionable recommendations if bullying is detected
